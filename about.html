Our Story & Vision

Preserving heritage, empowering community, and celebrating African creativity.

Afikpo Carnival & Resorts Ltd (AC&R)

Incorporated in August 2023, Afikpo Carnival & Resorts Ltd is dedicated to delivering culturally authentic, internationally-aligned festivals and tourism products.

Our programs prioritize cultural preservation, job creation, and inclusive local development while attracting sustainable investment to Afikpo and Ebonyi State.

Our Mission

"To celebrate and preserve the rich cultural heritage of Afikpo through world-class festivals and events that foster community pride, stimulate economic growth, and promote tourism across Africa and beyond."

Our Vision

"To become a leading cultural hub that transforms Afikpo into a premier destination for cultural tourism, driving social development and economic prosperity."
