Maiden Edition: December 2026

The Afikpo International Carnival (AIC) is a week-long cultural festival that showcases Afikpo’s living heritage, performing arts, and eco-tourism attractions.

Organized by Afikpo Carnival & Resorts Ltd in partnership with FMACCE, World Carnival Commission, and the United Nations, AIC 2026 will host local and international performers, exhibitors, and cultural ambassadors.
