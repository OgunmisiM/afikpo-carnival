Discover Ehugbo

Where history meets natural beauty.

A Culturally Resonant Town

Afikpo (Ehugbo) is rich with history, artistry, and natural beauty. With archaeological evidence reaching into the Neolithic era, the community traces its artistic roots to ancient Igbo civilizations.

Ancient Traditions & Afikpo Masks

Experience high cultural value artifacts preserved and displayed by the state tourism board. These sites and relics demonstrate Afikpo's long and proud cultural heritage.

Golden Sand Beaches

Explore the riverfront beaches in Ndibe, Ozizza, and Unwana. These scenic locations are ideal for canoeing, sport fishing, and viewing breathtaking sunsets.
